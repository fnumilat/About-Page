Fnu Milat

Fnu Milat was born on July 3rd 1994 in Kabul Afghanistan. After living his first two decades of his life in poverty and war, also serving along with the United States and Afghan Military Forces as an Interpreter and Cultural Advisor for almost 3 years he had an opportunity to be granted a Special Immigrant Visa and move to the United States on September 2014. He is living in Dallas Texas. Fnu Milat is grduated with an Associate of Applied Science in Business Administration from Remington College and currently pursuing his dream of becoming a Web Developer. He studies Full Stack Web Development at Lambda School. Fnu Milat also produces and shares Self Development related content throughout major social media platforms and trying to help people understand their purposes in life and help them to be better version of themselves everyday of their lives.
