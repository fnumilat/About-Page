About Fnu Milat

Fnu Milat was born on July 3rd 1994 in Kabul Afghanistan. After living his first two decades of his life in poverty and war, and serving along with the United States and Afghan Military Forces as an Interpreter and Cultural Advisor for almost 3 years he had an opportunity to be granted a Special Immigrant Visa and move to the United States on September 2014. He is living in Dallas Texas. Fnu Milat grduated with an Associate of Applied Science in Business Administration from Remington College and currently pursuing his dream of becoming a Web Developer. He studies Web Development at Lambda School. Fnu Milat also pursues his passion and trying to help people to understand who really they are, why are they here and what are their purposes through Social Media platforms by creating contents.
